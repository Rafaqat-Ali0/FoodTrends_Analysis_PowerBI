# Power BI Nutrition & Lifestyle Dashboard

---

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of dietary habits, nutritional intake, and their impact on lifestyle and health outcomes. Using Power BI, four interactive dashboards were developed to uncover patterns, correlations, and actionable insights related to nutrition and well-being.

---

## Dashboards

This dashboard provides insights into common dietary choices and average consumption patterns.

- Distribution of dietary habits

Understand general eating patterns and identify dominant food trends.

## Key Insights
- Dietary habits significantly influence overall health outcomes  
- Nutrient deficiencies are linked to specific diseases  
- Breakfast plays a critical role in daily nutrient intake  
- Lifestyle choices directly impact nutritional balance

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data-driven insights can guide healthier lifestyle and dietary decisions. The dashboards provide an intuitive and interactive way to explore nutrition-related patterns and their real-world implications.
